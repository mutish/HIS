About Us

Welcome to Afya Hospital — a trusted healthcare partner dedicated to the well-being of expectant mothers, newborns, and families. Our hospital provides a safe, supportive, and compassionate environment with a focus on personalized, high-quality maternity and pediatric care.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver holistic, patient-centered care to mothers and newborns throughout every stage of their journey. We aim to offer exceptional medical services with compassion, ensuring every family receives support from prenatal visits to postnatal care and beyond.

Our Vision

We envision a healthier future by becoming a leader in maternal and child healthcare. Through innovative practices, community education, and a family-first approach, we strive to uplift generations with quality care that inspires confidence and trust.

Our Services

At Afya Hospital, we proudly offer a wide range of services tailored to meet the needs of mothers, infants, and families:

  • Maternal Care: Comprehensive antenatal and postnatal support for a safe and healthy pregnancy journey.
  • Pediatric Services: Expert care for newborns and children, including check-ups, immunizations, and treatment for common conditions.
  • Labor & Delivery: Modern delivery suites equipped for comfort, safety, and a positive birthing experience.
  • Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU): Advanced, specialized care for premature and critically ill infants.
  • Breastfeeding Support: Guidance from certified lactation consultants to help new mothers establish successful breastfeeding practices.
  • Family Planning: Counseling and services to empower families with informed reproductive choices and fertility support.

Our Values

Our core values shape every aspect of how we deliver care and build trust with our community:

  • Compassion: We care for every patient with kindness, empathy, and dignity.
  • Excellence: We pursue the highest standards in clinical practice and patient outcomes.
  • Innovation: We embrace modern medical technology to enhance healthcare delivery.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty, transparency, and ethical responsibility.
  • Collaboration: We work as a team — with families and caregivers — to ensure comprehensive, personalized care.

Why Choose Us?

Choosing Afya Hospital means placing your family in capable, caring hands. Our dedicated team of professionals, state-of-the-art facilities, and commitment to compassionate service ensure you receive the best possible care — from pregnancy to parenthood and beyond.
